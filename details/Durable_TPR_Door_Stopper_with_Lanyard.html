Durable TPR Door Stopper with Non-Slip Base and Cowhide Lanyard

Keep your doors open safely and protect your space with this reliable TPR door stopper. Crafted from high-quality, reusable TPR material, it offers excellent shock absorption to prevent your doors from slamming shut. The non-slip rubber base ensures it stays firmly in place and safeguards your floors from scratches and dents over time, whether you're at home or in the office.

This door stopper is designed to fit doors with a thickness of up to 4.4cm and door gaps of 0.5~2cm, making it versatile for a wide range of door types.
